Featured Museums

The Louvre

Located in Paris, France, the Louvre is the world's largest art museum and a historic monument. Known for its iconic glass pyramid entrance, the museum is home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

The British Museum

Situated in London, England, the British Museum is dedicated to human history, art, and culture. Its permanent collection of eight million works is among the largest and most comprehensive in existence, covering every continent.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

Commonly known as the Met, this New York City museum is the largest art museum in the United States. Its collection includes over two million works, divided among seventeen curatorial departments.
